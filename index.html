Empowering girls, fostering equality, and driving community transformation in Garissa.

Who We Are

We are a community-driven organization dedicated to empowering girls and women in Garissa. Our focus is on education, gender equality, and providing the resources needed for them to thrive and lead.

Our Motto

Inspiring unity and unleashing the potential of every individual.

Our Vision

A society where every girl thrives, fulfilling her potential and contributing to the community.

Our Mission

Empowering girls and women in Garissa by promoting education, gender equality, and providing essential resources.

Our Programs

Educational Outreach

We host workshops and mentorship programs, partnering with schools to inspire and empower girls through education. Together, we can unlock potential and create a brighter future for everyone.

Advocacy Campaigns

By rallying community leaders, policymakers, and educators, we push for policies and practices that uplift girls, ensuring equality and opportunities for all.

Community Dialogues

We foster meaningful conversations within communities, addressing challenges and finding shared solutions to help girls and women thrive.

Health and Wellness

Our health initiatives provide essential workshops and resources, focusing on hygiene, mental health, and access to quality care for girls and women.
